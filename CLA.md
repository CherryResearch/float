# CLA Policy

Float accepts external contributions only if the contributor agrees to the
assignment terms in
[CONTRIBUTOR_ASSIGNMENT_AGREEMENT.md](CONTRIBUTOR_ASSIGNMENT_AGREEMENT.md)
before or with the contribution.

Accepted contributions are assigned to the project operator to the fullest
extent permitted by law. Contributors do not retain ownership of accepted
contributions, except where applicable law prevents assignment, in which case
the fallback terms in the agreement apply.

The project operator may reject or defer any pull request, patch, or other
submission until the agreement has been signed or otherwise affirmatively
accepted.
